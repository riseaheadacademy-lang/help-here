Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a CV?
+

We deliver your CV within 48 hours of receiving your details. For urgent requests, we offer express delivery within 24 hours — contact us on WhatsApp to arrange this. Rush orders may incur a small additional fee.

Do you host portfolio websites?
+

Yes! Every portfolio website package includes free hosting for the first year. We deploy your site on a reliable platform (Vercel or Netlify) and connect it to a custom subdomain. After the first year, hosting renewal is very affordable.

Are mock interviews conducted live?
+

Yes, all mock interviews are live one-on-one sessions conducted via Google Meet or Zoom. Sessions last approximately 45 minutes and include a detailed feedback report sent after the session. We schedule sessions at a time that works for you.

Which internship tracks do you cover?
+

We specialise in Software Engineering, Cloud & DevOps, and AI Engineering tracks. Our CV writing, interview packs, and mock interviews are all tailored to the specific requirements of these fields. We can also accommodate full-stack, mobile, and QA engineering tracks on request.

What if I'm not happy with the CV or portfolio?
+

We include free revisions with every package. If you are not satisfied with the initial delivery, simply let us know and we will revise until you are 100% happy. Your satisfaction is our priority — we won't stop until you love your materials.

How do I pay and get started?
+

Simply reach out to us on WhatsApp with your chosen package. We accept payments via bank transfer (Sampath, Commercial Bank), online banking, or cash. After confirmation, we will send you a brief questionnaire to gather your details and get started immediately.

Do I need to be from a specific university?
+

Not at all. We help students from all Sri Lankan universities and IT institutes — government universities, SLIIT, NSBM, Informatics, IIT, and more. Whether you are a first-year or a final-year student, we can help you build a career-ready profile.
